# Contributing

We are really happy that you want to contribute. To help you get started, please read this document.

## Tooling

Please use [rumdl](https://rumdl.dev) to lint and format your markdown. This is also checked in CI.
Also, spell-check your work using something like <https://languagetool.org/>. Currently, this is not done in CI. 

## Style

Our docs are supposed to be tutorial-like and beginner friendly. For technical details, explain them in the glossary at the bottom of a guide
or link to a source, if the reader wants to go down a rabbit hole that's fine. You are free to use colloquial language if that improves the flow of the text.

## AI policy

Please do not submit AI/LLM-generated writing. LLMs are useful for many thing, writing prose is not one of them.  
Our docs are written by humans who put a lot of effort into having a nice end result. Many readers can tell if writing was AI-generated and
that doesn't accurately reflect the time that goes into working on these docs. If we were to just have LLMs write our docs,
there is little benefit to the reader -- they might as well ask their LLM of choice instead of referring to our resources.
